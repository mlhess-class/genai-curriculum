Course Overview

This advanced course covers the design and implementation of production-grade AI systems. Students will work with multi-agent architectures, advanced retrieval-augmented generation, fine-tuning techniques, evaluation frameworks, and MLOps practices. The capstone project requires building a deployable multi-agent system evaluated against real-world benchmarks.

Prerequisite: GENAI 301 (Intermediate Generative AI) with grade B or higher

Format: 2× 75-min lectures + 1× 2-hr lab per week
