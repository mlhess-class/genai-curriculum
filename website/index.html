Curriculum

Five courses that take you from foundations to building complete AI solutions.

GENAI 101

Foundations of Generative AI

Explore the core concepts behind large language models, diffusion models, and transformers. Understand tokenization, embeddings, attention mechanisms, and the mathematical foundations that power modern AI.

3 CreditsFall Semester
GENAI 201

AI-Powered Workflows & Automation

Design intelligent automation pipelines using APIs, prompt engineering, and orchestration tools. Learn to integrate AI into business processes, from document processing to customer service workflows.

3 CreditsFall Semester
GENAI 301

Building with LangChain

Deep dive into the LangChain ecosystem — chains, agents, memory, and retrieval-augmented generation. Build sophisticated applications including chatbots, research assistants, and data analysis tools.

3 CreditsSpring Semester
GENAI 401

Advanced AI Systems & Architecture

Architect production-grade AI systems with a focus on scalability, evaluation, safety, fine-tuning, and multi-agent orchestration. Covers MLOps, deployment strategies, and responsible AI frameworks.

3 CreditsSpring Semester
GENAI 490

Capstone — AI Solution Design

Synthesize everything into a real-world capstone project. Partner with an industry sponsor to design, build, and present an end-to-end AI solution addressing a genuine business challenge.

3 CreditsSpring Semester

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

What are the admission requirements?▾
Applicants should hold a bachelor's degree in any field. Basic programming experience (Python preferred) is recommended but not required — we offer a free pre-program bootcamp to get you up to speed.
How long does the program take?▾
Most students complete the certificate in 8–12 months (two semesters). Part-time students can extend to three semesters. All courses are available in evening and online formats.
What is the tuition cost?▾
Total program tuition is $12,500 for the 15-credit certificate. Payment plans and employer tuition reimbursement assistance are available. Early applicants receive a $1,500 scholarship.
Can I take courses individually?▾
Yes. Each course can be taken as a standalone professional development course for $2,800. Credits earned apply toward the full certificate if you decide to enroll later.
Is this program available online?▾
Absolutely. All courses are offered in both in-person (evening) and fully online synchronous formats. Online students participate in the same lectures, projects, and capstone experience.
Do I need a computer science background?▾
Not necessarily. While technical aptitude helps, our cohorts include professionals from business, healthcare, law, and education. GENAI 101 is designed to bring everyone to a common foundation.
